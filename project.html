HACKING PREVENTION ON EMAIL USING MULTIFACTOR AUTHENTICATION

Email is still massively used standard communication channel. Messages send and receive through email are not secure and the content of messages are more prone to hackers. The main aim of this project is "To send and receieve mail with more Confidential and Authenticated".We enhances the multifactor authentication like username through which user can login , Face detection and signature to protect from hacking. Through this undertaking, we can make our own particular client id, sends to any client and oversee inbox. Any mail related report can be seen through the site. Undesirable or Spam messages are filtered automatically. Sending mail in Tamil is the additional feature added to our system.

SECURITY FEATURES


The user will usually be prompted to enter their email address and password. Once they have entered this information correctly, they will be logged in and granted access to the account.


Facial recognition technology is used to verify the identity of the user before allowing them to access their email account. This will prevent unauthorized access to the user's email account and reduce the risk of cyber attacks.

A digital signature is an electronic means of verifying the identity of the sender and ensuring that the message has not been tampered with or modified in transit. When an email is signed with a digital signature, it includes a unique digital certificate that identifies the sender and verifies the message's content.

SPAM ANALYSIS

In this system, to solve the problem of spam, the spam classification system is created to identify spam and non spam. Since spammers may send spam messages many times, it is difficult to identify it every time manually . So we will be using some of the strategies in our proposed to detect the spam. The proposed solution not only identifies the spam word but also identifies the IP address of the system. The spam messages is sent so that the next time when the spam message is sent from the same system our proposed system directly identifies it as blacklisted based on the IP address.
