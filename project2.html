CAR RESALE VALUE PREDICTION

The car price prediction has been a high interest research area, as it requires noticeable effort and knowledge of the field expert. Considerable number of distinct attributes are examined for the reliable and accurate prediction. The recent advent of online portals has facilitated the need for both the customer and the seller to be better informed about the trends and patterns that determine the value of a used car in the market.This model will help to predict the price of a used car, based on previous consumer data and a given set of features such as Kilometer,Speed,Fuel type Respective performances of different algorithms were then compared to find one that best suits the available data set.
